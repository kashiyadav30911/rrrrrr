## 🎯 Quick Navigation

Your app now has these pages:
- **Home** (`/`) - Landing page

## ⚡ 5-Minute Quick Start

### Step 1: Build Your Resume (2 minutes)
1. Go to http://localhost:3000
2. Click "Get Started Free"
3. Fill in your information:
   - Personal info
   - Summary
   - Education
   - Experience
   - Projects
4. Click "Save" to store locally

### Step 2: Use AI Skill Suggestions (1 minute)
1. In the Builder, go to "Skills" section
2. Click "AI Skill Suggestions" (purple button)
3. Enter your job role (e.g., "Frontend Developer")
4. Click "Generate"
5. Select skills you want
6. Click "Add X Skills"

### Step 3: Check Your Score (1 minute)
1. Click "Dashboard" in the top navigation
2. View your resume score (0-100)
3. Read improvement suggestions
4. See what sections are missing

### Step 4: Choose a Template (30 seconds)
1. Click "Templates" in navigation
2. Browse the 4 available templates
3. Click on one to preview
4. Click "Use This Template"

### Step 5: Track Job Applications (30 seconds)
1. Go to "Job Tracker"
2. Click "Add Application"
3. Enter company and position
4. Select status
5. Click "Add Application"

## 🎨 Feature Highlights

### 1. Dashboard - Your Resume Command Center
**What it does:** Shows your resume score, completion percentage, and improvement tips

**How to access:**
- Click "Dashboard" in the builder header
- Or go to `/dashboard`

**What you'll see:**
- Completion percentage (e.g., 85%)
- Resume score out of 100
- Section breakdown
- AI suggestions
- Missing fields alert

**Pro tip:** Aim for 85+ score for best results!

---

### 2. Template Gallery - Professional Designs
**What it does:** Browse and preview 4 professional resume templates

**Available templates:**
- ✨ **Modern** - Gradient header, eye-catching
- 💼 **Professional** - Two-column, executive style
- 📋 **Minimal** - Clean and simple
- 📄 **Classic** - Traditional ATS-friendly

**How to use:**
1. Go to `/templates`
2. Click any template to preview
3. Use "Fullscreen" for detailed view
4. Click "Use This Template" to apply

**Pro tip:** Modern and Minimal are most popular!

---

### 3. AI Skill Suggestions - Smart Recommendations
**What it does:** AI suggests relevant skills based on your target job role

**How to use:**
1. Builder → Skills section
2. Click "AI Skill Suggestions"
3. Enter job role
4. Select skills
5. Add to resume

**Supported roles:**
- Frontend/Backend/Full Stack Developer
- Data Analyst/Scientist
- DevOps Engineer
- Product Manager
- UI/UX Designer
- Any custom role!

**Pro tip:** Try different role variations for more suggestions!

---

### 4. Resume Score - Know Your Strength
**What it does:** Calculates a 0-100 score based on completeness and quality

**Scoring:**
- Summary: 20 points
- Experience: 30 points
- Education: 15 points
- Skills: 20 points
- Projects: 15 points

**Score meanings:**
- 85-100: Excellent ✅
- 70-84: Great ⭐
- 50-69: Good ⚠️
- 0-49: Needs work ❌

**Pro tip:** Add quantifiable achievements to boost your score!

---

### 5. Job Application Tracker - Stay Organized
**What it does:** Track all your job applications in one place

**Features:**
- Add unlimited applications
- Track status (Applied/Interview/Rejected/Offer)
- Add notes and details
- View statistics

**How to use:**
1. Go to `/job-tracker`
2. Click "Add Application"
3. Fill in details
4. Update status as you progress

**Pro tip:** Add notes about interview questions and feedback!

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### AI Not Working?
- Check `.env.local` has `OPENROUTER_API_KEY`
- Verify API key is valid
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page

### Data Not Saving?
- Click "Save" button in builder
- Check browser allows localStorage
- Try different browser
- Clear cache and retry

### PDF Not Downloading?
- Allow pop-ups in browser
- Check download folder
- Try different browser
- Ensure resume has content

### Score Not Updating?
- Go to Dashboard
- Refresh page
- Ensure resume is saved
- Check all sections are filled

## 📊 Understanding Your Score

### What Affects Your Score:

**Summary (20 points):**
- Length (150+ characters)
- Professional tone
- Clear career goals

**Experience (30 points):**
- Number of entries (3+ ideal)
- Detailed descriptions
- Quantifiable achievements

**Education (15 points):**
- At least one entry
- Complete information
- GPA if strong (>3.5)

**Skills (20 points):**
- 8+ relevant skills
- Mix of technical and soft skills
- Industry-specific keywords

**Projects (15 points):**
- 2+ projects
- Detailed descriptions
- Technologies used
- Links to demos/code
